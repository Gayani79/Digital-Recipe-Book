  1. Ingredients:
    • 1/2 portion Sweet Waffle Batter
    • 100ml Ice Cream
    • 30g Nutella

    Preparation Method:
    1. Pre-heat your waffle iron to its hottest setting and apply one surface of your waffle iron with butter.
    2. Ladle 90ml of waffle batter on the iron and close it.
    3. Bake for 4 minutes until the waffle is golden brown in colour.
    4. Lift the waffle out of the iron and transfer it to the cleaned breakfast serving plate.
    5. Apply 30 gm of nutella on the waffle.
    6. Add 100ml of ice cream over the ice cream and middle of the waffle.

  3. Ingredients:
    • 2 cups milk
    • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
    • 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
    • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 1/4 cup dark chocolate (chopped, optional for richness)

    Preparation Method:
    1. In a saucepan, whisk together sugar, cocoa powder, cornstarch, and salt.
    2. Gradually stir in milk until smooth.
    3. Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring constantly until it thickens and begins to bubble.
    4. Stir in chopped chocolate (if using) until melted.
    5. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract.
    6. Pour into serving dishes, cover with plastic wrap to prevent skin, and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
