  1. Ingredients:
    • 2 cups cooked rice
    • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, capsicum, etc.), finely chopped
    • 2 tablespoons oil or butter
    • 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
    • 1 small onion, finely chopped
    • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    • 1 teaspoon vinegar
    • 1 teaspoon black pepper powder
    • Salt to taste
    • Spring onions for garnish

    Preparation Method:
    1. Heat oil in a large pan or wok. Saut√© garlic and onions until fragrant.
    2. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry on high heat for 3-4 minutes.
    3. Add soy sauce, vinegar, black pepper, and salt. Mix well.
    4. Add cooked rice and toss until everything is well combined.
    5. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.

    • 1 tablespoon chili flakes
    • 1 tablespoon dried oregano

    Preparation Method:
    1. Heat the oven to 220¬∞C (425¬∞F).

    • 2 tablespoon olive oil

    • Lemon wedges (optional)

    Preparation Method:
    1. Heat olive oil in a pan, saut√© garlic, chili slices, and seafood until lightly cooked. Sprinkle paprika and chili flakes. Set aside.
    2. Heat oven to 220¬∞C (430¬∞F).
    3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the pizza base. Sprinkle half the mozzarella. Add the saut√©ed seafood, distributing evenly. Top with remaining mozzarella.
    4. Place pizza in the oven and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbling.

    • 2 tablespoon olive oil
    • 1 medium onion (chopped)
    • 2 carrots (sliced)
    • 2 celery stalks (sliced)
    • 3 garlic cloves (minced)
    • 8 cups chicken broth
    • 2 cups cooked chicken (shredded or diced)
    • 1 cup egg noodles (or any pasta)
    • 1 tablespoon dried thyme
    • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
    • Salt and pepper (to taste)

    Preparation Method:
    1. Heat olive oil in a pot. Saut√© onion, carrots, celery, and garlic until softened.
    2. Add chicken broth, thyme, and parsley. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 10 minutes.
    3. Add noodles and cook until tender (about 6-8 minutes).
    4. Stir in shredded chicken and heat through.
    5. Adjust salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot

  5. Ingredients:
    • 2 tablespoon butter
    • 1 small onion, finely chopped
    • 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
    • 250g mushrooms, sliced
    • 2 tablespoon all-purpose flour
    • 2 cups vegetable or chicken stock
    • 1 cup milk or cream
    • Salt and pepper, to taste
    • Fresh parsley (optional, for garnish)

    Preparation Method:
    1. Heat butter in a pot over medium heat. Saut√© onions and garlic until soft.
    2. Add mushrooms and cook until tender, about 5 minutes.
    3. Sprinkle flour over the mushrooms, stirring constantly to coat. Cook for 1-2 minutes.
    4. Gradually pour in the stock, stirring to prevent lumps. Bring to a simmer.
    5. Blend part or all of the soup for a creamier texture.
    6. Stir in milk or cream, heat gently without boiling.
    7. Add salt, pepper, and garnish with parsley if desired.
