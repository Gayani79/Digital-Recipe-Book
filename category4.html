Cakes


  1. Ingredients:
    • 200g digestive biscuits or graham crackers
    • 100g melted butter
    • 400g cream cheese(softened)
    • 200ml heavy cream(whipped)
    • 100g powdered sugar
    • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
    • Juice of 1 lemon(optional)
    • Fruit compote,fresh fruits,or chocolate syrup(as desired)

    Preparation Method:
    1. Crush biscuits into fine crumbs.
    2. Mix with melted butter until combined.
    3. Press firmly into a springform pan and chill in the fridge.
    4. Beat cream cheese,powdered sugar,and vanilla until smooth.
    5. Fold in whipped cream and lemon juice(if using).
    6. Spread the filling over the chilled base evenly.
    7. Smooth the top with a spatula.
    8. Refrigerate for at least 4-6 hours or overnight for the best set.


  2. Ingredients:
    • 1 3/4 cups(220g) all-purpose flour
    • 3/4 cup(65g) unsweetened cocoa powder
    • 2 cups (400g) granulated sugar
    • 1 1/2 tablespoon baking powder
    • 1 1/2 tablespoon baking soda
    • 1 tablespoon salt
    • 2 large eggs
    • 1 cup(240ml) whole milk
    • 1/2 cup (120ml) vegetable oil
    • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
    • 1 cup (240ml) boiling water

    Preparation Method:
    1. Preheat oven to 350F(175c).Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
    2. In a large bowl,sift together flour,cocoa powder, sugar,baking soda,and salt.
    3. Add eggs,milk,oil,and vanilla.Beat until smooth.
    4. Gradually stir in boiling water(batter will be thin).
    5. Divide batter evenly into pans.
    6. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
    7. Cool for 10 minutes,then remove from pans and cool completely.
    8. Frost with your favorite chocolate frosting.

    7. Let the cake cool completely. Dust with powdered sugar or drizzle with chocolate ganache, if desired.
