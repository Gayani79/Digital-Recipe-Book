    • 30ml blueberry puree
    • 160g ice cubes
    • 30g frappe powder
    • fresh milk
    • whipped cream

    Preparation Method:
    1. Add fresh milk,frappe powder,25ml of blueberry topping and ice cubes into blender jar according to given order and blend it in a high mode for 1 minute .
    2. Pour the mixer into serving glass and top up with 30ml whipped cream.
    3. Garnish with 5 ml blueberry topping and serve with smile.


  2. Ingredients:
    • 100ml fresh milk
    • 300ml vanilla ice cream
    • 30g nutella
    • 30g whipped cream
    • 3g chocolate chip

    Preparation Method:
    1. Add cold milk(Temp 1c-5c)to blender jar.
    2. Add vanilla ice cream and nutella ioto the blender and blend it on high mode for 1 minute.
    3. Pour the mixture into the pilsner glass and top it up with whipped cream and granish with chocolate chip.
    4. Sprinkle chocolate chips over whipped cream.

    • 1 cup brewed coffee (cooled)
    • 1/2 cup milk (or any preferred milk alternative)
    • 2 tablespoons sugar or sweetener (optional)
    • Ice cubes
    • Whipped cream or chocolate syrup for topping(optional)

    Preparation Method:
    1. Prepare coffee and let it cool to room temperature or refrigerate it.
    2. In a glass, add ice cubes, cooled coffee, milk, and sugar (if using). Stir well.
    3. Garnish with whipped cream or a drizzle of chocolate syrup.
