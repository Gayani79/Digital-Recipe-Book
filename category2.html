Lunch


  1. Ingredients:
    • 2 cups of cooked and cooled white rice(preferablr day-old)
    • 2 tablespoon vegetable oil
    • 2 cloves garlic,minced
    • 1 small onion,finely chopped
    • 1 red chili(optional),finely sliced
    • 2 eggs,beaten
    • 1 cuo of mixed vegetables(like peas,carrots,and green beans)
    • 1/2 cup cooked chicken, shrimp, or tofu (optional)

    Preparation Method:
    1. In a small bowl, mix kecap manis, soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), and white pepper. Set aside.
    2. Heat 1 tbsp of oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat. Add the beaten eggs and scramble until just set. Remove and set aside.
    3. Add another tablespoon of oil to the pan. Saut√© the garlic, onion, and chili until fragrant (about 1-2 minutes).
    4. Add the mixed vegetables and cooked chicken/shrimp/tofu. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until everything is heated through.
    5. Add the cooked rice to the pan, breaking up any clumps. Pour in the seasoning sauce and mix well, ensuring the rice is evenly coated. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
    6. Return the scrambled eggs to the pan and mix them with the rice. Cook for another minute.
    7. Transfer the Nasi Goreng to plates. Top with a fried egg, sliced cucumber, tomatoes, fried shallots, and lime wedges. Serve with prawn crackers on the side.

    • 1 Choice of Bagel
    • 10g Mayonnaise
    • 1 BBQ chicken portion
    • 1 BBQ sauce portion
    • 3 Tomato slices
    • 3 Onion rings

    Preparation Method:
    1. Heat oiled pan and add BBQ chicken portion and BBQ sauce portion with 15g of water and cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
    2. Slice bagel in half and apply mayonnaise in both slices.
    3. Place BBQ chicken on bottom slice of the bagel and add 3 slices of tomato and 3 rings of onion over the chicken.
    4. Close with the upper slice of the bagel.
    5. Add bagel into preheated oven and heat for 5 minutes and serve with a smile.

  4. Ingredients:
    • Cooked chicken(shredded or diced)
    • Lasagna sheet
    • Marinara or pasta sauce
    • Ricotta or cottage cheese
    • Mozzarella cheese(shredded)
    • parmesan cheese(grated)
    • garlic,onion,and olive oil
    • Egg(for cheese mixture)

    Preparation Method:
    1. Take the thawed lasagna out of the chiller, remove the cling wrap.
    2. Insert it into the lasagna serving bowl and put into the microwave and heat for 5 minutes.
    3. Take out from microwave and add 5 g of parmesan cheese on top.
    4. Place the microwave heated lasagna in the oven and bake for 9 minutes at a temperature of 250 degree Celsius.
    5. The crust of the lasagna top should be golden brown in color.
    6. Using a butter knife feel the temperature by dipping it into the center of the heated lasagna and placing the side of the knife on hand (reverse of your palm).
    7. Add dry oregano on top and garnish with piece of parsley.
