Breakfast


  1. Ingredients:
    • 2 slices choice of bread
    • 10g butter
    • 1 boiled egg
    • 20g mayonnaise
    • pinch salt and pepper

    Preparation Method:
    1. Take two slices of bread and apply butter.
    2. Grate boiled egg using a grater.
    3. Mix grated egg with mayonnaise,pinch of salt and pepper.
    4. Spread the egg mixer on the lower slice of the bread and close with the upper slice.
    5. Toast,sandwish for 45 second at 170 celsiaus in the griller or until golden brown colour lines appear. And the surface is crispy.


  2. Ingredients:
    • 280g chicken & mushroom sauce
    • 150g linguine pasta
    • 1g salt
    • 1g pepper
    • 50ml cooking cream
    • 2g coriander leaves
    • 5g parmesan cheese

    Preparation Method:
    1. Heat a pan over medium heat and add chicken pasta portion.
    2. Add 30 ml milk and cooke for like 1 minutes stirring occasionally.
    3. Meanwhile, Linguine pasta put into hot water & after 2 minutes strain it.
    4. Drain the pasta and add it to the sause and mix well with 2g chopped parsley.
    5. Transfer to a pasta plate and garnish with parsley and parmesan cheese on top,serve warm.

    • 2 slices choice of bread
    • 10g butter
    • 1 cheese slice
    • 2 ham slice

  4. Ingredients:
    • 1 butter croissant
    • 10g butter
    • 2 cheese slice
    • 5tomatoe slice
    • 1g salt and pepper

    Preparation Method:
    1. Carefully slices your croissant in half lengthways apply butter on both slices.
    2. Place two slices of cheese on bottom slice of the croissant and lace 5 slices of tomato over the cheese slices.
    3. Sprinkle pinch of salt and pepper over the tomato slices and close with the upper slice.
    4. Add croissant onto a baking tray and bake in the preheated oven for 6 minutes.

  5. Ingredients:
    • 1 (80g) Thai chicken portion
    • 1 (200g) Steamed rice
    • 1 portion carrot portion
    • 30g butter
    • 5g parsley

    Preparation Method:
    1. Heat a pan with 30g of butter and add finely chopped parsley.
    2. Add rice portion to the pan and cook on low heat for 2 to 3 minutes while mixing well.
    3. Transfer rice portion to pasta plate.
    4. Heat a pan and add thai chicken portion and carrot portion.
    5. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes and transfer to the plate as shown in picture.
    6. Garnish with a piece of parsley.
